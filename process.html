Process Page

Welcome to the process page detailing the creation of my website.

In this page, I will provide a comprehensive overview of the various phases involved in the design and development of the site. From initial planning and inspiration to wireframing, graphic design prototyping, and development, I'll walk you through the journey of bringing this project to life. Each phase will be discussed in detail, highlighting key decisions, methodologies, and tools utilized along the way. Let's dive into the process of crafting a user-friendly and visually appealing website.

Planning Phase

The primary goal of creating my website was to establish a dynamic online presence that effectively showcases my work, skills, and expertise while providing valuable resources and information to visitors.
Portfolio page: To present a comprehensive portfolio of my projects, achievements, and experiences in a visually appealing and organized manner. The website serves as a showcase of my skills, talents, and capabilities to potential clients, employers, and collaborators.
Contact page: To foster engagement and interaction with visitors through various interactive elements such as contact forms and social media integration.
Resume page: To provide visitors, including potential employers, clients, collaborators, and industry peers, with a concise yet detailed snapshot of my career journey and expertise.

Rationale

My rationale is professional visibility. In today's digital age, establishing a strong online presence is essential for professionals across various industries. By creating a website, I aim to enhance my visibility and accessibility to potential employers, clients, collaborators, and industry peers. A website serves as a centralized platform where interested parties can learn more about my skills, experiences, and achievements.

Inspirations

The creation of my website was inspired by a combination of diverse influences, design principles, and industry trends. These inspirations guided the conceptualization, design, and development of the website, shaping its overall aesthetic, functionality, and user experience. Some key inspirations include:
Minimalism and Simplicity: The ethos of minimalism and simplicity served as a foundational inspiration for the design of my website. Drawing inspiration from minimalist design principles, I aimed to create a clean, uncluttered layout that prioritizes clarity, readability, and visual hierarchy.
User-Centered Design: User-centered design principles inspired the focus on creating a website that prioritizes the needs, preferences, and behaviors of its users.
Typographys Trends: Inspirations from typography and visual design trends informed the aesthetic direction of the website. I drew inspiration from contemporary typography styles, font pairings, and graphic design trends.

WCAG2.0 Accessibility

Here is my statement and summary of measures taken to ensure WCAG2.0 Accessibility.

Statement

In designing my website, I have prioritized accessibility in accordance with WCAG 2.0 standards. I recognize the importance of ensuring that individuals with disabilities can access and navigate my site effectively. My approach to accessibility involves a comprehensive consideration of diverse user needs, encompassing factors such as limited vision, hearing impairments, cognitive limitations, mobility challenges, and more.

Alt Text for Images

Every image on my website includes descriptive alt text, providing users with screen readers or those with low vision a comprehensive understanding of the visual content.


Semantic HTML

I've structured my website using semantic HTML elements, ensuring proper heading hierarchy, labeling form inputs, and employing landmark roles such as nav, main, and footer for improved navigation and screen reader interpretation.

Color Contrast

I've meticulously selected color palettes and ensured sufficient color contrast ratios between text and background elements, meeting WCAG standards to accommodate users with color vision deficiencies.


Video and Form Accessibility

For videos featured on my website, I've provided access to closed captions to make content accessible to users who are deaf or hard of hearing. Additionally, I've incorporated controls for adjusting playback speed and volume. Forms on my website include descriptive labels and fieldsets.

Accessible Links and Buttons

Links and buttons are designed with clear and descriptive text labels, enhancing their accessibility for users relying on screen readers.
